ABOUT GAME

Let's keep the winter story short and sweet here.

Winter Star welcomes you into a a winter night scene where the snow catches the light just right, keeping that cozy holiday feeling going even after the decorations come down. Perfect for folks who actually love winter (yes, we exist!) and want to keep that seasonal magic going way past New Year's.

You'll recognize the usual symbols, but they've got this cool icy makeover. The fun stuff though? That's in the special features: Wilds, Golden Bells, Scatter Pays, and a unique Multichoice Bonus Mode that lets players shape their own winter journey.
